Rolex

History

Rolex was founded in 1905 by Hans Wilsdorf and Alfred Davis in London, England. The company's original name was Wilsdorf and Davis Ltd.

Location

The company moved to Geneva, Switzerland in 1919 due to high taxes and import duties on luxury goods in the United Kingdom.

Logo

The Rolex crown logo was registered in 1931 and is now one of the most recognized logos in the world. The five points of the crown represent the company's commitment to quality, innovation, reliability, style, and heritage..
